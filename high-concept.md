# High Concept - Make It Out

## Elevator Pitch

`Make It Out` is a fast, tense maze-escape experience where each run presents a new procedural layout, forcing players to make quick route decisions under pressure to survive and find the exit.

## Player Fantasy

You are trapped, disoriented, and racing against danger. Every turn matters, and confidence in your route is your best weapon.

## Core Experience Goals

- Immediate control clarity.
- High tension with readable challenge.
- Strong "one more run" replay loop.

## Target Session Length

- 3 to 8 minutes per run.

## Scope Guardrails

- Prioritize a complete, polished core loop over feature breadth.
- Defer non-essential systems until core playtest feedback is stable.
